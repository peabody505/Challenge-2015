Our Mission

Keys to People was created to help anyone and everyone who is and has been victimized by the practice of human trafficking. When we first came up with Keys to People, we had some choices. We decided to go with Human Trafficking, because it was something that was important to all of us. We all knew about it, and that it was still happening, but not until we did more in depth research did we really know the scope of the problem. Human trafficking, a 150 billion dollar industry, second only to drug trafficking, is a major issue, and must be stopped.

Our Team

The four of us, Rufus, Lainey, Rose, and Alex, created Keys to People because human trafficking was an issue we all wanted gone. We don't belive going around the world help people is the best way to solve the problem. There's only so much we can do. We think that the best way to go around the problem is to educate the public. If they know how to solve it, and proavtively do so, it will be done with much quicker, and with less effort on everyone's part. Why try to lift a 2 ton rock yourself when there are many others who will help you? This is our mentality, and it's why we can do what we do.
